Timeline of 2020



2020 is something we wish not to remember,
but still there is alot of things that we probably forgot











  • Australian bushfires

    The county faced its most devastating wildfires, it burned a record 47 million acres, displaced thousands of people and killed at least 34 people.

  • January 1, 2020. 12 am (UAE)

    RAK Sets a World record

    Ras Al Khaimah New Year's Eve set two Guinness World Records - for the 'Longest chain of fireworks' and for the 'Longest straight line of fireworks'.

  • 9th January, 2020

    COVID-19 pandemic

    The World Health Organization announced Jan. 9 that a deadly coronavirus had emerged in Wuhan, China. In a matter of months, the virus has spread across the globe to more than 20 million people, resulting in at least 751,000 deaths.

  • 26th January, 2020

    Kobe Bryant's death

    The legendary Los Angeles Lakers player passed away along with his daughter, Gianna, and seven others when their helicopter crashed in Calabasas, Calif., on Jan. 26.

  • May 26, 2020

    BLM Protests

    The police-involved killings of George Floyd, Ahmaud Arbery and Breonna Taylor sparked a wave of peaceful — and sometimes violent —riots across the world to demand an end to police brutality and racial injustice.

  • May 29, 2020

    Murder hornets

    The invasive insect, known as the Asian giant hornet, was spotted for the first time stateside when they invaded Washington state. Measuring up to two inches, the hornets can wipe out entire bee colonies within hours.

  • July 15, 2020

    Twitter hack

    A group allegedly spearheaded by a Florida teen took over the Twitter accounts of prominent business and political figures — including Joe Biden, Bill Gates, Elon Musk and Kanye West — in a bitcoin scam.

  • August 28, 2020

    Chadwick's death

    “Black Panther” actor Chadwick Boseman died at age 43 after a four-year battle with colon cancer. His death on Aug. 28 shocked fans, since he had completed several blockbuster films while keeping his diagnosis private.

  • 14 December 2020

    COVID vaccine rollout

    The first Covid-19 vaccination in the United States took place, as the country geared up for its largest ever immunisation campaign.

  • Jan, 1st 2021, 12am (UAE)

    35-min fireworks

    The world’s first-ever Girandola fireworks show and the largest straight-line display, filled citizens and residents with fresh hope and optimism. All that packed in a 35-minute long spectacle.
